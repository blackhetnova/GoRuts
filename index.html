Terms & Conditions

Surat BRTS Digital Ticketing Guidelines 2026

1. Digital QR tickets issued via Sitilink/GoRutes are valid for 2 hours from ticket issuance time.

2. Passengers must scan the QR code at the station smart entry gate before boarding the bus.

3. Suman Pravas passes allow unlimited rides across all Surat BRTS lines until 10:00 PM on purchase date.

4. Tickets are non-transferable and must be presented to ticket inspectors upon request.

Privacy Policy

Surat Smart City Development Ltd. Data Policy

1. All passenger travel preferences and ticketing history are stored locally on your device for fast offline access.

2. No personal location data is tracked or transmitted to external third-party servers.

3. Contactless payment transactions are encrypted using official UPI QR & Bank standards.
